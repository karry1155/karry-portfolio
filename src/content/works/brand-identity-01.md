A complete brand identity system for a minimalist lifestyle studio. The visual language centers on geometric reduction and precise typographic hierarchy.

## Brief

Design a cohesive visual identity that communicates restraint, quality, and intentionality. The brand needed to work across print, digital, and environmental applications.

## Process

Started with an extensive typographic exploration — testing 40+ typeface pairings before landing on a custom-modified sans-serif for the logotype.

The color system uses a restricted palette of off-white, deep charcoal, and a single accent that shifts across seasonal campaigns.

![Logotype exploration](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1558618666-fcd25c85cd64?w=800&q=80)

## Deliverables

- Primary and secondary logotype
- Color system and usage guidelines
- Typography scale
- Business card, letterhead, packaging mockups
- Brand guidelines document (42 pages)
