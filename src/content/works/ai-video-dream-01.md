A 90-second looping video piece exploring threshold spaces — airports, hotel corridors, empty malls at 3am.

## Approach

Generated using a combination of Sora for initial motion and Runway Gen-3 for detail passes. Each scene was prompted iteratively over ~200 generations before selecting final takes.

The audio is a custom composition using sustained tones and field recordings from actual liminal spaces.

## Technical Notes

- Base generation: Sora (1080p, 24fps)
- Refinement: Runway Gen-3 Alpha
- Color grade: DaVinci Resolve
- Audio: Ableton Live

Total generation time: ~40 hours across 3 sessions.
