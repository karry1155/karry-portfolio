A stylized reimagining of Shanghai's skyline through AI video generation — pushing hyperrealistic urban textures into something surreal.

## Process

Shot reference footage across 3 nights in Pudong. Used this footage as ControlNet input to Kling v1.5, maintaining camera movement while transforming the visual language entirely.

Final piece runs 3 minutes and was commissioned for a brand activation campaign.

## Stack

- Reference capture: Sony A7IV
- Generation: Kling v1.5 + ComfyUI pipeline
- Upscaling: Topaz Video AI
- Grade: DaVinci Resolve
